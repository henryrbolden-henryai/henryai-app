What's Included

Fit Analysis

We score your match against every job using a 50/30/20 model: 50% skills, 30% experience, 20% scope/seniority. You get a clear number, not vague encouragement.

Reality Check

Market context that most candidates never see: how competitive this role is, what salary range to expect, and red flags in the job posting.

Screening Question Guidance

Many applications auto-reject based on screening questions. We show you which answers matter and why.

Interview Prep Guides

Company-specific preparation with likely questions, talking points, and what they're really evaluating at each stage.

Mock Interviews

Practice with an AI interviewer trained on real interview patterns. Get scored feedback on your answers.

Pipeline Command Center

Track every application with status, priority, and recommended next actions. See your whole search at a glance.

Cross-Interview Pattern Analysis

After 3+ interviews, we analyze patterns across all your debriefs. Find out if you keep stumbling on the same question types.

Story Bank

Curate your STAR stories and track which ones land. Know which stories to tell and which ones to retire.

Rejection Forensics

Analyze your rejection patterns to find the leak in your funnel. Are you getting rejected at resume, recruiter, or HM stage? We'll tell you why.

Negotiation Prep

Comp benchmarks for your target role, talking points for the conversation, and strategy for counter-offers.

Live Coaching Sessions

45-minute sessions with a human coach. Strategy, role-play, gut checks. For when you need judgment, not just data.

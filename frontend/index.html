How we work together

1

Start with your story

You begin by sharing your resume so I can translate it into a clear and structured view of your experience and strengths. My role is to help you see your impact and capture the parts of your story that matter most to hiring teams.

2

Clarifying the opportunity

When you share a job description, I interpret the role the way an experienced recruiter or hiring manager would. I look past generic bullet points to find the real priorities and understand where the role sits inside the business.

3

Building your application strategy

For each opportunity, I help you decide how to position your experience and which strengths to highlight. You receive resumes and cover letters tailored to the specific role along with guidance that keeps everything true to you but clearer and more strategic.

4

Showing up confidently at each stage

As you move forward, I support you through outreach, interview preparation, transcript reviews, and next step planning. The goal is to help you stay confident, keep improving, and move through the process with intention instead of guesswork.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account and data?

Contact us and we'll process your request within 48 hours. All your personal data will be permanently removed.

Is my resume data stored securely?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and secure database services. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Contact us to request a full export of your account data including your profile, generated documents, and application history.

What if I disagree with a fit assessment?

Our assessments are designed to be honest, not discouraging. If you believe an assessment is missing context about your experience, you can always apply anyway. The assessment is guidance, not a mandate.

# Technical Individual Contributor Leveling Framework

## Overview
This framework defines career progression for technical individual contributors (Software Engineers, Data Engineers, ML Engineers, Platform Engineers, DevOps/SRE, etc.) using standard industry titles. All roles follow a pure IC track with no management requirement.

## Level Structure

| Level | Title | Typical Time in Role | General Scope |
|-------|-------|---------------------|---------------|
| **Entry** | Engineer I / Software Engineer I | 1-2 years | Small independent tasks within projects |
| **Mid** | Engineer II / Software Engineer | 2-3 years | Full project ownership |
| **Senior** | Senior Engineer / Senior Software Engineer | 3-5 years | Cross-team influence |
| **Staff** | Staff Engineer | 3-5 years | Multi-team domain expertise |
| **Senior Staff** | Senior Staff Engineer | Open-ended | Company-wide technical vision |
| **Principal** | Principal Engineer | Open-ended | Industry-defining innovation |
| **Distinguished** | Distinguished Engineer / Fellow | Open-ended | Industry leadership |

## Level Definitions

### Engineer I / Software Engineer I

**Typical Experience:** 0-2 years  
**Common Alternative Titles:** Junior Engineer, Associate Engineer, Software Engineer I

**Summary**  
Focus on delivering assignments with high code quality, design resiliency, and timely delivery. Build a strong foundation for future success. Expected progression: 1-2 years.

**Scope**  
- Work on small independent tasks (2-8 weeks)
- Inherently curious with appetite for learning
- Establish relentless work ethic and passion for quality
- Build understanding of project context and dependencies

**Impact**  
- **Technical:** Work impacts immediate team's deliverables
- **Cultural:** Colleagues view them as reliable with strong integrity

**Competencies**

*Code Quality & Documentation*  
Demonstrate strong understanding of assigned codebase, product area, and systems. Actively make recommendations to improve codebase, product, or systems.

*Architecture & Design*  
Technical design has positive long-term impact. Understand defensive programming, consider edge cases and thoughtful error handling. Participate in design reviews. Pay careful attention to testing, documenting, and progressive rollouts.

*Problem Solving*  
Think of solutions that are memory, I/O, and compute efficient. Display creative thinking, enabling the team to innovate.

*Delivery*  
Complete most work on schedule. When unable, communicate circumstances appropriately with reasonable explanations.

*Productivity*  
Stick to timelines, sometimes delivering earlier. Help other team members ensure overall project delivery. Always available for code reviews and design reviews.

*Growth Mindset*  
Understand and work to improve based on constructive feedback.

*Communication*  
Actively collaborate with Tech Lead or manager to develop new areas of expertise. Proactively communicate on task results, issues, and progress updates.

---

### Engineer II / Software Engineer

**Typical Experience:** 2-4 years  
**Common Alternative Titles:** Software Engineer, Software Engineer II

**Summary**  
Deliver broader and deeper contributions to projects with critical thinking beyond what is asked. Make larger impact while maintaining code resiliency, design thoroughness, and timely delivery. Expected progression: 2-4 years.

**Scope**  
- Make contributions to project beyond specific features
- Show curiosity beyond current technologies, constantly evaluating pros/cons
- Identify better ways to achieve goals, leading innovation
- Move with purpose and intention

**Impact**  
- **Technical:** Work directly impacts team success. Project delivery depends heavily on their efforts. Teamwork leads to highly reliable services with high-quality code.
- **Cultural:** Influence surrounding peers, gaining respect through collaboration, thoughtful design, and commitment to completion.

**Competencies**

*Code Quality & Documentation*  
Demonstrate strong engineering skills. Design code that is solid for long-term. Understand how systems interact with broader technical stack.

*Architecture & Design*  
Technical design has positive long-term impact. Pay careful attention to testing, documenting, and progressive rollouts.

*Problem Solving*  
Identify, drive, and lead changes as needed. Exercise priority and discretion when raising issues.

*Delivery*  
Sustained self-driven productivity. Exercise independent judgment in driving solutions. Complete medium to large features independently without guidance.

*Productivity*  
Stick to timelines, sometimes delivering earlier. Always available for code and design reviews. Part of emergency response when needed. Absorb unplanned work effectively.

*Growth Mindset*  
Actively pursue personal development opportunities related to career growth.

*Communication*  
Evangelize work product among relevant teams. Collaborate effectively with other teams. Understand organizational processes for releases, outages, and launches.

### Senior Engineer / Senior Software Engineer

**Typical Experience:** 5-8 years  
**Common Alternative Titles:** Senior Software Engineer, Senior Engineer

**Summary**  
Think beyond project boundaries. Broader cross-organizational awareness. Work influences change affecting one or more adjacent organizations. Identify gaps in tools, technologies, and products. Naturally emerge as point of contact for their area.

**Scope**

*Scope of Work*  
Propose new solutions to existing problems, improving scalability and reliability. Work with peers and cross-functional teams for meaningful contribution. Articulate vision to technical and management leaders. Possess deep technical knowledge in at least one domain. Gain recognition among cross-functional groups. Don't stop at project boundaryâ€”investigate adjacent dependencies for larger impact.

*Coaching and Mentoring*  
Approached by less experienced engineers for guidance on technical matters and culture. Earn peer respect through thoughtful contributions.

**Impact**  
- **Technical:** Create significant impact in delivery and code quality in own and surrounding teams. Sought for feedback on new services. Architect and lead technical vision for projects. Work impacts adjacent teams. May lead small projects or teams.
- **Cultural:** Help manager mentor junior engineers. Impact broader than just code. Influence culture of team and adjacent teams.

**Competencies**

*Code Quality & Documentation*  
Review design and architecture of dependent codebase to ensure overall efficiency.

*Architecture & Design*  
Expert end-to-end understanding of systems within domain across company.

*Problem Solving*  
Act as influencer, proactively finding problems without direction. Independently make suggestions to correct problems. Drive to resolution regularly without guidance.

*Delivery*  
Consistently high productivity across many projects. Track record of successfully completing large scope projects timely and efficiently.

*Productivity*  
High impact on project and adjacent projects by going beyond project responsibilities. Rapidly familiarize with codebase to juggle multiple responsibilities.

*Growth Mindset*  
Drive tasks across other teams. Build relationships throughout company. Demonstrate empathy. Proactively seek feedback on improvement.

*Communication*  
Involved in preparing and delivering large communication efforts (emails, presentations, blog articles). Provide detailed constructive feedback to improve team performance.

### Staff Engineer

**Typical Experience:** 8-12 years  
**Common Alternative Titles:** Staff Software Engineer, Staff Engineer

**Summary**  
Deep experts across multiple disciplines. Question status quo. Constantly raise standards for scalability, availability, and reliability. Create durable systems while understanding all aspects of technology and business. Enable operational efficiency for multiple key domains.

**Scope**

*Scope of Work*  
Established as go-to engineer in multiple domains. Aware of many aspects of company including entire technical stack. Effectively make risk decisions impacting business. Identify needs for better technology, find solutions, socialize them, and make them part of core stack.

*Coaching and Mentoring*  
Actively mentor senior engineers through 1:1 interactions. Contribute to overall engineering mentor programs.

*External Influence*  
Respected outside company. If working on open source, one of lead contributors on well-known projects. Invited to conferences. Publishing papers and authoring detailed blog articles.

**Impact**  
- **Technical:** Identified key gap in platform or infrastructure. Worked with leadership to fund initiative. Defined success of large, complex project and delivered in phases.
- **Cultural:** Leading teams through influence, steering in clear direction. Others seek advice on technical and non-technical matters.

**Competencies**

*Code Quality & Documentation*  
Review multiple codebases, identify large technology gaps, and articulate them. Define urgency/priority of creating new technical components.

*Architecture & Design*  
Understand capabilities and limitations of existing technology. Explain how this translates into business opportunities and risks. Help company avoid bad technologies and decisions. Aware of government/policy regulations impacting operations.

*Problem Solving*  
Detailed understanding of company goals and strategy. Use influence to galvanize teams to solve problems. Understand how organization works organizationally and technically.

*Delivery*  
"Go-to" person for one or more business areas. Track record as person who gets big things done effectively and swiftly.

*Productivity*  
Move needle for company by rolling out features/products/tools that 10x the solution for key problems. Pick areas that cannot be solved by junior or mid-level engineers.

*Growth Mindset*  
Exceptional at mentoring. Demonstrate maturity. Deal effectively with conflict. Trusted advisor for teams and leaders.

*Communication*  
Communicate across product group and entire company. Describe complex features/systems in detail to both technical and non-technical audiences.

### Senior Staff Engineer

**Typical Experience:** 12-16 years  
**Common Alternative Titles:** Senior Staff Software Engineer, Senior Staff Engineer

**Summary**  
Visionary architects. Work has impact on multiple aspects of business. Deep insights leveraged to innovate completely novel approaches, creating new precedence in field. Great teachers, mentoring everyone. Notable external presence in industry.

**Scope**

*Scope of Work*  
Established as world-class expert in multiple domains. Known inside and outside company as leader in field. Define company's future for several years. Constantly identify ways to make whole bigger than sum of parts.

*Coaching and Mentoring*  
Natural coach and mentor. Selflessly contribute back to society beyond company.

*External Influence*  
Author well-received blog posts. Invited to speak at major conferences. Lead open source projects. Spread insight and work throughout industry.

**Impact**  
- **Technical:** Impact visible throughout engineering, product, and business. Blend technology and business. Technical expertise led to creating unique value proposition. Engineers beyond company using their technology.
- **Cultural:** Work with management piloting new programs. Identify ways to increase technical density and integrity.

**Competencies**

*Code Quality & Documentation*  
Write code used as example by other engineers. Build new technical components from ground up.

*Architecture & Design*  
Deep, expert level, industry-leading experience in one or more domains. Technical vision and implementations exceptional. Master in debugging extremely difficult technical problems.

*Problem Solving*  
Solve problems for company while introducing solutions adoptable beyond company. Often see problems and opportunities no one else sees.

*Delivery*  
Exemplary in actions, ensuring high bar. Visibility spans across engineering.

*Productivity*  
Track record of leading and delivering seemingly impossible projects across many functional areas.

*Growth Mindset*  
Impact beyond company. Drive technical and process changes across entire company. Nothing gets in their way.

*Communication*  
Able to explain all of engineering: where headed, what priorities are in technology, where to focus energy.

### Principal Engineer

**Typical Experience:** 16-20+ years  
**Common Alternative Titles:** Principal Software Engineer, Principal Engineer

**Summary**  
Pioneers in field. Work brings technology to next level throughout industry. Known in industry as world expert. May serve on technical advisory boards at other companies. Shape future of company's technical systems.

**Scope**  
World-class experts in many domains. Lead technical advisory boards or participate at other companies. If working on open source, lead top projects. Communications to technical public received with interest and considered noteworthy contribution.

**Impact**  
Impact clearly measurable and visible. Helped company in many directions: improving thinking around technology adoption, improving business metrics, contributing to culture. Day-to-day work moves needle on core metrics.

**Competencies**

*Code Quality & Documentation*  
Write/review code across stack. Dig into any part of technical stack and identify areas of improvement.

*Architecture & Design*  
Technical work unparalleled. Can build and implement technology and systems no one else can. Skills recognized internally and externally.

*Problem Solving*  
Arrive at solutions to problems no one else can, due to ability to rethink problems from first principles.

*Delivery*  
Vision and perseverance combine to overcome almost any obstacle.

*Productivity*  
Output extreme and awe-inspiring example.

*Growth Mindset*  
No such thing as insurmountably hard problem. Break through almost any technical challenge. Bring others along, making them better.

*Communication*  
Explain technical problems and solutions to top-level executives. Articulation of gnarliest technical problems to non-engineering executives.

---

### Distinguished Engineer / Fellow

**Typical Experience:** 20+ years  
**Common Alternative Titles:** Distinguished Engineer, Engineering Fellow, Technical Fellow

**Summary**  
Highest technical level. Industry-defining contributions. Shape direction of technology field. Represent company as technical authority globally.

**Scope**  
Industry-recognized expert. Define state of the art in field. Shape industry standards and direction. Represent company in highest-level technical forums.

**Impact**  
Transformational impact on company and industry. Work cited and studied by others. Fundamental contributions to field of computer science or engineering.

**Competencies**  
Operate at highest levels of technical excellence. Recognized globally as authority in domain. Contributions shape how entire industry thinks about problems.

## Progression Guidelines

### Typical Progression Timelines

**Fast Track (Top 10%):**
- Engineer I: 0-1 years
- Engineer II: 1-2 years  
- Senior Engineer: 3-4 years
- Staff Engineer: 6-8 years
- Senior Staff: 10-12 years
- Principal: 15+ years

**Standard Track (Majority):**
- Engineer I: 0-2 years
- Engineer II: 2-4 years
- Senior Engineer: 5-8 years
- Staff Engineer: 10-13 years
- Senior Staff: 15-18 years
- Principal: 20+ years

**Important Note:** These are guidelines only. What matters is demonstrated competency at the target level, not years of experience.
